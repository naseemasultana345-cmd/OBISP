Biography

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was one of India's greatest scientists and leaders. He was known as the “Missile Man of India” because of his important role in developing India’s missile and space technology.

Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Dr. Kalam came from a simple family background. Through dedication, hard work, and education, he became one of the most respected personalities in the country.

In 2002, he became the 11th President of India and inspired millions of students and young people with his speeches, books, and vision for a developed India.

Dr. Kalam believed in dreaming big and working hard to achieve success. His life continues to motivate people across the world.
